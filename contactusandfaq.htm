How long does each filter last?

Each filter cartridge will last for 6,000 litres.

An averaged sized family typically consumes 15 – 25 litres of water a day. So a filter cartridge will last for about 6 – 12 months, depending on usage.

The ionizer will prompt when your consumption hits exactly 6,000 litres and you can change it yourself then. Each filter takes around 5 minutes to change.
