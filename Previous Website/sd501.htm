LeveLuk SD501

Released in 2007, SD501 has been our flagship model. It exhibits high performance with 7 electrode plates that efficiently produce 5 types of water to serve the daily needs of your household.

The plethora of functions means that health, hygiene and eco-friendliness can be achieved altogether to produce the safest home for your loved ones.

The clear liquid crystal display and voice prompts will inform you of the kind of water being produced and necessary information, such as notifying you that filter has to be changed after every 6000 litres of water is used.
