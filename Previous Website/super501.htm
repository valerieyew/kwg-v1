Super 501

Built with “elite” in mind, the LeveLuk Super 501 represents the pinnacle of its class. Housing a total of 12 electrode plates, the LeveLuk Super 501 comes with all the bells and whistles of the SD501 while possessing a higher flow rate.

This machine is built for extra heavy duty use and is also capable of producing Strong Acidic water (pH 2.5) and Strong Kangen water (pH 11.5) much more efficiently.

It is most suitable for users who require extensive use of above two types of water, such as restaurant and factory owners who need them to clean raw food or sanitize equipments.

Specifications

  • 12 electrode plates
  • Display: Large-sized LCD
  • Voice prompt
  • Language: English
  • Warranty: 3 years
